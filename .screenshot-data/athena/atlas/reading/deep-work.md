# Deep Work - Cal Newport

## Definition
Deep Work: Professional activities performed in a state of distraction-free concentration that push your cognitive capabilities to their limit.

## Key Strategies
- **Time blocking** - Schedule deep work sessions
- **Shutdown ritual** - Clear end to work day
- **Embrace boredom** - Don't reach for phone at every idle moment

## Rules
1. Work deeply
2. Embrace boredom
3. Quit social media
4. Drain the shallows

## My Implementation
- Morning block 6-9am for deep work
- Phone in another room during focus time
- Weekly review of time spent on shallow vs deep
