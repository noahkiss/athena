# Gardener Classification Rules

## Categories

- **projects/** - Work and personal projects, goals, deliverables
- **journal/** - Daily reflections, mood logs, personal observations
- **people/** - Contact notes, meeting summaries, relationship notes
- **tech/** - Programming tips, tool configurations, technical references
- **reading/** - Book notes, article summaries, quotes
- **wellness/** - Health tracking, workout logs, habits
- **home/** - Household tasks, maintenance, shopping lists
